Privacy Policy

Bible Fun Quiz - Your Privacy Matters to Us

Introduction

Bible Fun Quiz ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Bible Fun Quiz mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including quiz scores, progress, and preferences.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device, such as device type and operating system version.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App functionality
  • Save your progress and quiz scores
  • Improve and optimize the App experience
  • Respond to your requests or questions

Data Storage and Security

Your quiz progress and scores are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store any personal information on our servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

Our App does not use third-party services that collect information about you. We do not share your information with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our App is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App's content is focused on Bible education and does not require or collect any personal information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the App
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the App
  • Contact us with questions about your privacy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us
