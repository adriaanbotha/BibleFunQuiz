Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

You can reset your progress by going to Settings > Reset Progress. Note that this action cannot be undone.

Is the app available offline?

Yes! Bible Fun Quiz works offline. We store a set of questions locally so you can play anywhere, anytime.

How do I change difficulty levels?

You can change difficulty levels from the main menu. Tap on the difficulty level you want to play.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes, you can use the app on multiple devices. Your progress will sync across devices when you're online.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Please email us at support@biblefunquiz.com with details about the bug or your feature suggestion.

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues, please try the following steps:

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Restart the app
  4. If the issue persists, contact our support team with details about:
    • Your device model and OS version
    • Steps to reproduce the issue
    • Screenshots if applicable

Privacy and Data

We take your privacy seriously. Our app:

  • Stores data locally on your device
